Welcome to the Customer Overview page of my CRM system!

This page serves as a central hub for managing your contacts efficiently. Here's a simple guide on how to navigate and utilize this page:

Adding Contacts:
To begin, click on the "Add Contact" button, located on the bottom right corner of the page. Fill in the necessary details such as the person's name, contact information, and any additional relevant data. Once you're done, hit "Save" to add the contact to your list.
Viewing Contacts:
The main section of the page displays a list of all your contacts. You'll find essential information like names and key details here. Scroll through this list to get a quick overview of your contacts.
Editing Contacts:
If you need to update any information for a specific contact, simply click on their name in the list. This action will take you to an editable view of their details. Here, you can make changes, corrections, or updates as necessary. Don't forget to hit "Save" after making your edits.
